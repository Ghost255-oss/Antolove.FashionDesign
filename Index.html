THE ATELIER

Cloth, colour, ceremony.

Antolove was founded in Gezina, Pretoria to honour the wardrobes of South African celebration — the umembeso, lobola, the white wedding and the graduation. Each commission begins with a conversation about story, family and the cloth that carries them.

We work with Shweshwe, Xibelani pleating, isicholo forms, beading and contemporary formalwear — fitted to the body and the day.
